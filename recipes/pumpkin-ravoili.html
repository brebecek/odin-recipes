Pumpkin Ravioli in Brown Butter Sauce

Home

Description

This dish will wow you or your guest with little effort. The sauce will come together on its' own, and if you mess up the water to ingredient ratio, you can fix it right up!

Ingredients

  • 9 oz or so of pumpkin ravioli
  • 4 tbsp of butter
  • 25 g of pecans
  • 6 fresh sage leaves
