Description

These Dino Nuggets remind me of childhood. I only make this page because some people I know struggle to follow the back-of-the-bag instructions, and thats' okay. Perhaps, it's nice to know that someone we love has our back. I have your back.

These pair perfectly with Kraft Mac n Cheese or your favorite creamy, saucy noodle by the way.

Ingredients

  • Your favorite bag of Dino Nuggets
  • Your sauce of choice. Mine is spicy ketchup

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees for soft nuggets, or 425 for crispy nuggets.
  2. Onnce preheated, for softer nuggets, cook for 15 minutes. For crispy nuggets, cook for 25 minutes.
