Dino Nuggets

Ingredients

  • Your favorite bag of Dino Nuggets
  • Your sauce of choice. Mine is spicy ketchup

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees for soft nuggets, or 425 for crispy nuggets.
  2. Onnce preheated, for softer nuggets, cook for 15 minutes. For crispy nuggets, cook for 25 minutes.
